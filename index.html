About Us

Welcome to Restoran

GTRBurger is a business entity that represents the Ruminant Generation Transformation Club Cooperative Malaysia Berhad which represents 5000 members including ruminant breeders such as cattle, goats, deer and dairy cows.

GTRburger promotes local beef and mutton products as downstream products to the public. We also open opportunities for all Malaysians, who are qualified to join us in building the GTRBurger business nationwide We want to build a GTRBurger empire with you all. Do not miss this opportunity!
